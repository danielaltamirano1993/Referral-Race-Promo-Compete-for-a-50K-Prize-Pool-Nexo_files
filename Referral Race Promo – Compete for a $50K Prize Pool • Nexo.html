FAQs

How long will this promo last?

This limited-time promo will be available only from Wednesday, May 26 at 00:00 UTC to Monday, July 26 at 23:59 UTC.

How do I invite friends to join Nexo?

You can start inviting friends as soon as you create your Nexo account.

  1. Log in to your account and make sure Identity Verification is completed.
  2. Copy the unique link in your profile from the Referral tab.
  3. Share it with a friend, ask them to create a Nexo account, and complete Identity Verification.
  4. Once they register and top up at least $100 in supported assets, you’ll both get $25 in BTC after a 30-day period passes.

You both will be able to see the bonus transaction in your Nexo accounts – however, its status will be pending.

Example:

Let’s say you refer a friend on June 5, and your friend completes identity verification and tops up $100 on June 10. That means you’ll both get rewarded $25 worth of BTC by July 10.

Note: The reward will be credited to your balance, provided that the portfolio of your invited friend is still worth more than $100 at the end of the 30-day period.

To learn more about Nexo's Referral Program, visit our Help Center.

How many friends can I refer?

There’s no limit on the number of people you can refer – and you’ll get $25 in crypto for each referral. The only condition is that your friends haven’t created a Nexo account before – only brand new customers are accepted.

How to keep track of my referrals?

You can track the current status of all your invited friends in the Referral tab inside the Nexo app.

How and when will the $50K prize pool be distributed?

The $50,000 prize pool in NEXO Tokens will be distributed among the top 50 users who have referred the most friends.

Mid-Term Competition Results Board

As the promotion continues for 2 months, there will be three mid-term checkpoints on which we will share a leaderboard with the top 50 participants who have made the most referrals up to this moment.

  • Checkpoint 1: June 15, 2022
  • Checkpoint 2: June 30, 2022
  • Checkpoint 3: July 15, 2022

The final leaderboard containing the full list of winners will be announced by August 30, 2022. The prize pool will be distributed between the winners by September 1, 2022.

Note: Rewards for US residents or citizens will be paid out in BTC.

I didn’t get my referral reward. Why?

You can always sign in to your account and check your reward status. Here are some reasons why you didn’t get your referral reward:

  • Your invitee must sign up directly after opening your unique link. Referrals are not captured if the invited person signs up using via the Nexo app and not your link.
  • If your friend fails to complete the referral instructions within 90 days of signing up, you won’t receive the reward.
  • Your invitee needs to pass Identity Verification and hold the equivalent of $100 or more in any of the supported assets for at least 30 days.
  • The portfolio of your referred friend has to remain above $100 at the end of the 30-day period for the bonus to be awarded.
  • The balance of $100 should be sent from an external wallet. Participants who received internal transfers from other Nexo wallets will not be eligible for the Referral Program.

Keep in mind that once a friend tops up, you will both get your rewards within 24 hours after the 30-day period is over.

Note: Instances of one external wallet funding multiple Nexo accounts will lead to disqualification from the Referral Program ($25 reward and $50,000 Prize Pool) for both the referrer and the referral.
